What information we collect

We collect information in two ways: information you give us directly, and information collected automatically when you visit this site.

Information you provide directly may include:

  • Your name, email address, and phone number when you contact us or book a discovery call
  • Information about your career situation that you share during coaching sessions
  • Payment information processed through PayPal or other payment platforms

Information collected automatically may include:

  • Pages visited and time spent on this site
  • Browser type, device type, and general location

This site may use cookies and analytics tools such as Google Analytics to collect and analyze usage data. You may decline non-essential cookies through the cookie consent banner on this site.

How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Schedule and conduct discovery calls and coaching sessions
  • Send confirmation emails and session-related communications
  • Process payments for coaching services
  • Improve this website and understand how visitors find and use it
  • Respond to questions or inquiries you send us

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

How we store and protect your information

We take reasonable precautions to protect the information we collect. Coaching session notes and client information are stored securely and accessed only by Ty Smith. Payment information is handled directly by PayPal or other payment processors and is not stored on our systems.

This site may use third-party tools including Google Analytics, Squarespace, PayPal, and scheduling platforms such as Calendly or Acuity. Each of these services has its own privacy policy governing how they handle your data. We encourage you to review those policies directly.

You have the right to request access to the personal information we hold about you, to request that we correct or delete it, and to opt out of any marketing communications at any time. To make any of these requests, contact us at ty@hrexperttogo.com.
