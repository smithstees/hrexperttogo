About the discovery call

What actually happens on a discovery call?

We spend about 20 minutes talking through your current situation. Where you are in your search, what you have tried, and what feels stuck. From there I will give you a clear recommendation on what would actually help and whether working together makes sense.

It is not a coaching session. It is a focused conversation to make sure we are a good fit before either of us commits to anything.

Is the discovery call really free?

Yes. There is no charge and no obligation. If we are a good fit, I will walk you through next steps and what working together looks like. If we are not, I will tell you that honestly and point you toward something that might be a better fit.

What should I have ready before the call?

Just come with a clear sense of where you are and what feels most stuck. If you have a current resume, have it nearby in case it comes up. You do not need to prepare anything formal. This is a conversation, not an interview.

About the coaching

Will you write my resume for me?

No. Resume coaching at HR Expert to go is about working through your resume together so you understand how to position your experience clearly and speak directly to what recruiters and hiring managers are looking for. The words stay yours. That is intentional, because you are the one who has to stand behind them in an interview.

How is this different from just searching for career tips online?

Generic advice is written for everyone, which means it is optimized for no one. What you get here is someone who has spent 20+ years inside the hiring process looking specifically at your situation, your resume, your answers, and your goals, and telling you what actually needs to change.

Do you work with people outside of North Carolina?

Yes. All sessions are conducted virtually by video call. Where you are located does not matter.

Can you guarantee I will get a job?

No, and anyone who tells you they can is not being straight with you. What I can do is help you become the strongest candidate you can be. Hiring decisions involve factors outside anyone's control. What we can control is how prepared, how clear, and how confident you are going in.

About fit and pricing

Who is HR Expert to go for?

College students preparing for internships or full-time roles, recent graduates navigating a competitive market, and early-career professionals ready for their next move. If you are further along in your career and looking for executive coaching, this is probably not the right fit and I will tell you that on the discovery call.

How much does coaching cost?

Pricing is discussed on the discovery call based on what you actually need. Services are offered as focused packages rather than open-ended hourly sessions, so you know what you are getting and what it costs before you commit.

What if I am not sure which service I need?

That is exactly what the discovery call is for. You do not need to figure that out ahead of time. We will figure it out together.
