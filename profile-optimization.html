What to expect

Your profile should be working for you while you sleep.

Professional profile coaching is not about making your profile look busy. It is about making sure the right people can find you and that what they find gives them a reason to reach out.

What we cover

  • Your headline and whether it is positioned for visibility or just a job title
  • Your summary section and how well it communicates who you are and what you bring
  • How your experience descriptions read to someone scanning quickly
  • Skills and keywords that affect whether you show up in searches
  • What may be missing or hurting your visibility
  • Overall positioning for the roles you are targeting

What this is not

  • Rewriting your profile for you
  • Managing your account or posting content on your behalf
  • Messaging recruiters or making connections for you
  • A guarantee that recruiters will reach out

Why you make the updates

The same reason we do not write your resume for you. Your profile needs to sound like you. When a recruiter reaches out and you get on a call, your profile and your voice need to match. Coaching helps you understand what to change and why so you can make it yours.

Is this right for you

Profile coaching works best when you are ready to make the updates yourself.

You are a good fit if

You have a professional profile that exists but you are not sure if it is helping or hurting you. You want honest feedback and are willing to make the updates yourself based on what you learn.

Also a good fit if

You are starting your job search and want to make sure your profile is positioned correctly before you start applying so you are not invisible to recruiters who are actively looking.

Come prepared with

Your current profile and a sense of the roles you are targeting. If you have not set up a profile yet, we can also talk through how to approach it from scratch.

Not sure yet

Start with a free discovery call. We will figure out whether profile coaching, a broader package, or something else entirely makes the most sense for where you are.
