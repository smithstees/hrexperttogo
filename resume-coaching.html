What to expect

A conversation, not a critique.

Resume coaching at HR Expert to go is not about handing your resume off and getting it back rewritten. It is about working through it together so you understand how a recruiter reads it and what you can do to make it stronger.

What we cover

  • How your resume reads to someone reviewing it quickly
  • Whether your experience is positioned for the roles you are targeting
  • How your accomplishments are coming across and whether they are specific enough
  • Keywords and language that align with what hiring managers are looking for
  • Gaps, inconsistencies, or anything that might raise questions before an interview
  • Structure and clarity so your most relevant experience gets noticed first

What this is not

  • Resume writing or rewriting on your behalf
  • Formatting or design services
  • Submitting your resume to employers for you
  • A guarantee of interviews or callbacks

Why we do it this way

A stronger resume is only part of the goal. You also need to understand your own story well enough to explain it in an interview. Working through your resume together means you leave with both a better document and the confidence to back it up.

Is this right for you

Resume coaching works best when you are ready to do the work.

You are a good fit if

You are a college student, recent graduate, or early-career professional who wants honest feedback on your resume and is willing to make the changes yourself based on what you learn.

You may need something different if

You are looking for someone to write or rewrite your resume for you. That is a different service and not something HR Expert to go provides.

Come prepared with

Your current resume, the types of roles you are targeting, and any specific feedback you have already received that you want to address.

Not sure if you need this

Start with a free discovery call. We will look at where you are and whether resume coaching, a broader package, or something else entirely is the right next step.
