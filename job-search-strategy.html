What to expect

A search built around you, not a generic checklist.

Job search strategy coaching is about understanding where you want to go and building a realistic, organized plan to get there. We look at your target roles, your approach, and what may be getting in the way.

What we cover

  • Clarifying your target roles, companies, and industries
  • How to prioritize your search so you are focused, not scattered
  • Where to look and how to use job boards effectively
  • How to get your materials in front of people beyond just applying online
  • Networking for people who do not feel comfortable networking
  • How to track your search so nothing falls through the cracks
  • Follow-up strategy and how to stay organized through a longer search

What this is not

  • Applying to jobs on your behalf
  • Contacting employers or recruiters for you
  • A guarantee of responses or interviews
  • A placement or recruiting service

Why strategy matters

A focused search with 20 well-targeted applications tends to outperform a scattered search with 200. The goal is not to apply to everything. It is to get your materials seen by people who are actually hiring for what you do.

Is this right for you

Job search strategy coaching works best when you are ready to commit to a consistent effort.

You are a good fit if

You are actively searching or about to start and want to build an organized, focused approach from the beginning rather than figuring it out as you go.

Also a good fit if

You have been searching for a while without results and want an honest assessment of what may not be working and how to adjust your approach.

Come prepared with

A sense of the roles you are targeting, where you have been applying, and what the search has looked like so far. The more specific you can be, the more useful the session will be.
